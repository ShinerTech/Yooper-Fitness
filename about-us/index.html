About Us

Our Mission

"Our mission is to empower individuals to achieve their health and fitness goals through community, education, and personalized training. We strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome and supported on their journey to a healthier lifestyle."

The Story of Yooper Fitness

We opened our doors in March 2021 with one goal in mind—to create a welcoming space where everyone in our community can show up, work hard, and crush their goals! Whether you’re a seasoned athlete, just starting your fitness journey, or looking for a fun way to stay active, we’ve got something for you.

From top-notch equipment to dynamic classes and expert trainers, we're always evolving to bring you the best. We love seeing our members push limits, celebrate wins (big and small), and build a community that feels like family.

At Yooper Fitness, we're more than just a gym—we're a family. So come as you are, put in the work, and let’s get stronger together!

Meet the Owners

Tiger and Emily’s journey started at young ages, but their shared passion for health and community truly took off after graduating from Finlandia University with Nursing degrees. Life took them on adventures to Colorado and Minnesota before they returned to their roots in the U.P. after a decade away.

Along the way, they built a family—welcoming their children Bauer, Cooper, and Ryann. While settling into their new home in Manistique, they opened Yooper Fitness. Their vision to bring accessible, high-quality fitness to the U.P. kept growing, leading to the launch of additional locations in Manistique, L’Anse, Newberry, Houghton, and Gladstone.

From day one, Tiger and Emily have built Yooper Fitness on the values of community, hard work, and a passion for helping others. Whether you’re just starting out or pushing your limits, they’re here to support you every step of the way!
