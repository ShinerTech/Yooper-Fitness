The Yooper Fitness Experience

We are Yoopers who have our finger on the pulse of what it's like to live in the UP. We are open 24/7 for the hard-working people who live here. We understand how important it is to be available for those who do shift work or just need to get their workout in whenever possible, even if it's the middle of the night.

We try to offer services to educate and enhance the experience of the gym and are here to help!
